The Best Movies According To Hannah

My Top 3 Movies I Can Remember. :)

1. The Gooonies

Set in the gorgeous locale of Astoria, Oregon, a group of young friends find a treasure map and embark on an epic adventure to find the missing treasure and save their homes from destruction.

2. Overboard.

Another movie set in Oregon, this one is about a rich woman who loses her memory and ends up being duped into believing she's the poor wife of a local handyman who she wronged. Hilarity ensues as she learns how to take care of 4 rowdy boys and a wild husband. A feel good, romantic comedy for the whole family. (Plus a super cute hound dog for extra good movie bonus points!)

3. Crash Landing On You.

A South Korean romantic drama about a South Korean heiress who accidentally paraglides into North Korea and meets a North Korean army officer. The two fall in love despite the political tensions and cultural differences, leading to a heartwarming and suspenseful story that has captured the hearts of many viewers worldwide.
